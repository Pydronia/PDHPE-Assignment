45% of all young Australian injury related deaths are from road crashes.

As a learner driver, it is important to understand the road rules and be constantly aware of the situation. It can otherwise cost you your life. This campaign is aimed at learner drivers in order to educate and inform of the dangers of driving and encourage safe driving practices.

The biggest killer of young drivers is speeding.

An increase in speed by just 5km/h will result in a 15% increase in crashes. Slow down and sit below the speed limit. They're there for a reason.
